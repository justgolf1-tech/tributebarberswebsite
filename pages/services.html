Our Grooming Services — Haircuts, Shaves, Facials & More

Every service at Tribute comes with more than you'd expect — and nothing you didn't ask for.

Your service provider is matched to a tier based on their tenure, growth, and expertise with Tribute. That's what drives the price — not hidden add-ons.

Every visit includes a complimentary beverage (beer, wine, coffee — your call), a t-shirt to change into, and a shoe shine. You'll also get a hot towel with a custom fragrance, a shampoo and scalp massage, and a handful of other details you'll discover in the chair. It's all included. No surprises.

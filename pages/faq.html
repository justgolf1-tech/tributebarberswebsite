Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Book By Phone?

Absolutely. Call us at 303-224-9933 and we'll get you on the books.

Guarantee — Details

First-time guests — we're so confident you'll love your experience that your satisfaction is guaranteed or your money back. Requests must be made within 14 days of your visit. First-time guests only, determined by management, and the refund covers the price paid at the time of service.

How Can I Cancel?

You can cancel up to 24 hours before your scheduled time with no issues. If something comes up last minute, call our Guest Services team at 303-224-9933 and we'll take care of it.

I'm Late, What Do I Do?

We get it — traffic in the Tech Center is its own beast. We'll make every effort to stay on schedule and be respectful of the next guest's time. All we ask is that you show the same courtesy. We reserve the right to shorten or reschedule a service following a late arrival.

Cancellation Policy

Please give us at least 24 hours' notice so we can offer your time to another guest. Cancellations made within 30 minutes of the scheduled time — or no-shows — may be charged up to 100% of the service price.

Parties & Special Events?

Tribute hosts private events for every occasion — weddings, birthdays, corporate outings, you name it. Your group arrives, grabs a beer, and relaxes in the lounge while everyone gets dialed in for the big day.

Email us at tributebarbers@gmail.com to start planning.

No-Show Policy

We don't require a credit card on file unless you no-show two visits within a three-month span. That's a pretty generous window.

After two no-shows, we'll take a card to hold future bookings. Any no-show after that gets charged 100% of the service price. Our providers' time is their income — we're just protecting it. Makes sense, right?

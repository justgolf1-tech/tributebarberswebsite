Why Tribute?

We built Tribute for the people who push our world forward. The entrepreneurs, the builders, the doers, the ones who make things happen — this place is for you.

This is our way of saying thank you. We honored you by creating a barbershop where you actually feel like you belong.

Every detail of your visit is included. Complimentary beverage — beer, wine, coffee, whatever you want. A t-shirt to throw on so you don't spend the rest of your day covered in hair. A shoe shine while you're in the chair.

Then it goes further. A shampoo and scalp massage. A hot towel with a custom fragrance. And from there, your provider weaves in even more details that'll have you leaving here a different person than when you walked in.

Hidden fees, monthly recurring charges, and à la carte extras are silly — that's not our thing. It's tacky.
